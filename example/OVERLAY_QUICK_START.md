# Quick Start Guide: Screen Overlay

## What is the Screen Overlay?

The Screen Overlay is a floating widget that appears on top of all your apps, showing:
- Your current activity
- Number of connected devices
- Connected users and their activities

Think of it like a "picture-in-picture" for your connection status!

## How to Enable

1. Open the Nearby Connections example app
2. Scroll down to find the **"Enable Screen Overlay"** button
3. Tap the button
4. If this is your first time:
   - Android will ask for permission to "Display over other apps"
   - Tap "Allow" or toggle the permission on
   - Go back to the app
   - Tap **"Enable Screen Overlay"** again
5. The overlay should now appear on your screen! ğŸ‰

## How to Disable

1. Open the app
2. Tap **"Disable Screen Overlay"**
3. The overlay will disappear

You can re-enable it anytime!

## Tips & Tricks

- **Can't see it?** Make sure you granted the permission and re-enabled the overlay
- **In the way?** Just drag it to a corner or edge of your screen
- **Want it always on?** Keep the overlay enabled and switch between apps freely
- **Privacy?** The overlay only shows what you're already sharing with connected devices

## Troubleshooting

**"I don't see the overlay after enabling"**
- Check if you granted the permission in Settings
- Try disabling and re-enabling the overlay
- Restart the app

**"The permission request doesn't appear"**
- Go to Settings â†’ Apps â†’ Nearby Connections â†’ Display over other apps
- Manually enable the permission

**"The overlay disappeared"**
- You may have disabled it accidentally
- Just tap "Enable Screen Overlay" again

**"The overlay isn't updating"**
- Make sure you're connected to other devices
- Check that "Live Activity" is also enabled
- Try changing your activity to see if it updates

## What's Next?

Once you have the overlay enabled:
1. Connect to other devices using "Start Advertising" or "Start Discovery"
2. Change your activity using "Select Activity" or "Typing Activity"
3. Watch the overlay update in real-time!
4. Try using other apps while keeping an eye on the overlay
